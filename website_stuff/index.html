Welcome to the Rivals Racing League's Official Website

Here you can find the up-to-date points standings for the current season, and you can view a race report of the most recent RRL event. Below you can view the Weekly Update.

The Rivals Racing League was created in 2018 as an online racing platform for drivers from across the world to compete in at various track in multiple types of race cars. Currently, 48 seasons of RRL competition have been completed. We are currenlty on Season 49, and there are 6 races left in the season.

New Winner at Talladega

Superspeedways are known by race fans for two things: chaos and unusual winners. Last weekend's race at Talladega Superspeedway more than delivered on those expectations, as TheRoadRunner scored his first career win in the Rivals Racing League. Series point leader pemcraft28 grabs a second-place finish, pushing him ahead of JakeM706 for the first time this season.

League Info

This league uses Nascar Racing 2003 Season as its platform for hosting events. The game can be downloaded through many online websites, and all necessary additions can be found in the league's discord server.

How to Join

You can contact RRL staff through discord. Send a join request to the server, and admins will be review you request within two days. If your membership request is approved, you will be sent a paypal link in discord. A membership fee of $5 is require each season you compete in.
